Main Tactics

Here are the most basic tactics for the game:

They not very smart, but everyone must know about them

Zonal Marking
is a defensive football tactic where players defend specific zones rather than marking individual opponents.
Man-to-Man Marking
is a defensive football tactic where each defensive player is assigned to mark a specific opponent, following them wherever they go on the field.
Counter-Attacking
is a football tactic where a team quickly transitions from defense to offense in response to an opponent's attack, aiming to exploit open spaces and catch the opponent off guard.
